Lincoln's 10 Greatest Accomplishments

  1. he became the sixteenth president of the united states on march 4th, 1861
  2. lincoln is the only president of the united states to hold a patent
  3. he signed the first of the homestead acts, allowing poor people to obtain land
  4. he established the united states department of agriculture
  5. he signed the morrill land-grant act, which lead to the creation of numerous universities
  6. lincoln is behind the progressive nature of income tax in the us
  7. abraham lincoln issued the emancipation proclamation which led to abolishing slavery in the us
  8. lincoln established the us national banking system
  9. he led the union to victory in the american civil war
  10. he lay the steeping stone for reconstruction
